Behind the interface

How the private pipeline works

The production build operates on private exports; only this generalized process is public.

01
Extract

Walk nested export payloads and recover candidate post text, timestamps, authors, and canonical identifiers.

02
Deduplicate

Merge repeated captures by stable source key while retaining the strongest text and evidence metadata.

03
Classify

Score subject relevance and themes, then distinguish direct mentions from context-only candidates.

04
Review

Require a person to validate chronology, evidence quality, privacy, and the final public-safe summary.
