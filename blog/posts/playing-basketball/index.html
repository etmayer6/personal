Playing basketball lately

A short field note about spending some time on the basketball court and sharing the ordinary things happening away from a screen.

Outside workBasketball

Lately, I have been playing basketball.

That may be a smaller update than a new project or a major milestone, but that is part of what I want this blog to capture. Not everything happening in my life needs to turn into a polished case study. Sometimes the update is simply that I have been getting away from a screen and onto a basketball court.

Basketball asks for a very different kind of attention than software work. The game keeps moving, decisions happen quickly, and there is not much value in dwelling on the last possession when the next one has already started. It is a physical, immediate activity in a way that most of my projects are not.

There is also something satisfying about an activity with such clear feedback. A pass connects or it does not. A shot drops or it misses. The score changes, the next possession begins, and the game keeps moving.

I have not included details about where I have been playing, who I have been playing with, or how any particular game went because I have not shared those details yet. Those can become their own story later. For now, this note is here to record one uncomplicated fact: basketball has been part of what I have been up to lately.

This blog is meant to make room for updates like that, too.
